Rail Baltica Ülemiste terminal area desgined Zaha Hadid Architects
Structural Engineer | GRK Finland Oy

Lead Structural Engineer for railway infrastructure electrification. Engineering signaling and catenary structures with type drawings approved by Estonian authorities. Led weekly coordination meetings, managed BIM documentation, and conducted site visits to assess existing structures and validate designs. Developed temporary support solutions to maintain railway operations during construction. Delivered complete plan packages including structural calculations, 3D models, shop drawings, and bills of materials using AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Robot Structural Analysis, and Trimble Connect.

Electrification of Aegvidu - Tapa - Tartu in Estonia
Structural Engineer | GRK Finland Oy

Designed catenary and support structures. Produced type drawings for approval, maintained bills of materials, and performed modelling, FE-analysis, shop drawings, and calculations using AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and Robot Structural Analysis. Reviewed and approved structural reports and type drawings.

About the project:
Electrification project included spanning roughly 150 km of railway between Aegviidu, Tapa, and Tartu in Estonia. The scope included replacing existing catenary structures, developing new standardised type solutions for Eesti Raudte, and modifying existing structures where neccessary.

Pantograph Monitoring System (PMS) Gantry Design
Structural Engineer | GRK Finland Oy

Designed a signaling gantry bridge supporting four PMS units over four railway tracks. Coordinated closely with Mermec Group to ensure compatibility and integration. Engineered modular spans meeting strict equipment specifications such as oscillation and dynamic performance requirements. Delivered full structural documentation along with BIM models.

About the project:
GRK was assigned to upgrade and expand Pantograph Monitoring System (PMS) coverage across the Finnish railway network, requiring new structural gantries for several PMS units per gantry. The PMS units—supplied and installed by Mermec Group.
